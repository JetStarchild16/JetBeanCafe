About JET Bean Cafe

Where coffee culture meets otaku passion.

Founded in 2024, JET Bean Cafe was born from a simple idea: why can't you enjoy your favorite latte while browsing the latest anime figures?

Our founder, an avid anime collector and coffee enthusiast, wanted to create a space where fans could feel at home. A place where you can discuss the latest episode of your favorite show while sipping a perfectly crafted matcha latte.

Our Promise

  • ☕ Premium Coffee: Sourced from ethical farms
  • 📦 Authentic Merchandise: Only licensed and high-quality items
  • 🏠 Community Space: Events, screenings, and meetups
  • 🌱 Sustainable Practices: Eco-friendly packaging and ingredients
